Ginisang Sardines

A hearty Filipino dish featuring tender sardines simmered in a rich, savory tomato sauce with softened garlic, onions, and tomatoes. The dish is often garnished with sautéed vegetables and topped with a fried egg. Perfectly balanced with tangy and savory flavors.

Tinapa Fried Rice with Sardines

A savory dish of garlicky fried rice mixed with smoky tinapa flakes (shredded, smoked fish) and tender sardines, ideally in olive oil. Garnished with crispy fried garlic and fresh scallions, this hearty combo delivers a rich, comforting taste of the sea in every bite. Perfect for breakfast or any meal when you crave something deeply satisfying.

Sardine Pasta

A simple and quick dish, often made with canned sardines, garlic, olive oil, chili flakes, lemon, and parsley, tossed with spaghetti or any pasta of your choice. It’s light, flavorful, and perfect for a quick meal.

Sardines on Toast

An easy, classic dish with sardines served on crispy toasted bread. The sardines can be mashed with mustard, lemon juice, and herbs, or left whole. Finished with a sprinkle of pepper or chopped onions, it's a quick, flavorful snack or light meal. For an elevated touch, try adding a soft-boiled egg or a drizzle of olive oil on top.

Sardine Salad

A healthy option where sardines are mixed with fresh vegetables, dressed with olive oil, vinegar, and herbs. For the best results, use sardines packed in olive oil for added richness. This vibrant and zesty dish is perfect for a nutritious lunch or a side that pairs well with any meal.

Sardine Fritters

Crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, these fritters combine sardines with flour, eggs, onions, and herbs. Fried to golden perfection, they make for a savory snack or appetizer that's full of flavor. Perfect for dipping or enjoying on their own.

Sardine Sinigang

A comforting Filipino sour soup made with a tamarind base, featuring sardines in water or brine, tomatoes, and a mix of fresh vegetables. The tangy, savory broth pairs perfectly with steamed rice, making it a heartwarming meal full of bold flavors.

Sardine Croquettes

Crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, these croquettes are made with tender sardines packed in oil, mashed potatoes, and herbs. The savory blend of flavors makes them a perfect snack or appetizer that’s sure to satisfy.

Sardines with Rice and Egg

The ultimate no-fuss comfort food. Grab a tin of sardines (in oil or tomato sauce, your call), crack an egg, and plop it all on a bed of rice. Simple, satisfying, and always there when you need it.

Sardine Lumpia (Spring Rolls)

Crispy, golden-brown spring rolls filled with a mix of boneless, skinless sardines, vegetables, and glass noodles. Each bite offers a perfect blend of textures, complemented by a sweet and spicy dipping sauce that adds just the right kick.

Sardines and Crackers

A simple yet elegant snack featuring sardines in olive oil paired with crispy crackers. Perfect for adding to a fancy charcuterie board alongside cheeses, olives, and fruits. Enjoy with a crisp Sauvignon Blanc or a light, dry rosé to elevate the experience, or opt for sparkling water with a hint of citrus for a refreshing non-alcoholic option.

Sardines à la Can

Why mess with perfection? Crack open that tin, and you've got yourself a meal. It’s the culinary equivalent of “don’t fix what ain’t broke” – a classic, straight from the can, enjoyed in its natural habitat. Fork optional.
